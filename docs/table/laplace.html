Bảng Công Thức Laplace

Hàm số \(f(t)\) Biến đổi Laplace \(F(s) = \mathcal{L}\{f(t)\}\)
\(1\) \(\frac{1}{s}\), \(s > 0\)
\(t^n\), \(n \in \mathbb{N}\) \(\frac{n!}{s^{n+1}}\), \(s > 0\)
\(e^{at}\) \(\frac{1}{s - a}\), \(s > a\)
\(\sin(bt)\) \(\frac{b}{s^2 + b^2}\), \(s > 0\)
\(\cos(bt)\) \(\frac{s}{s^2 + b^2}\), \(s > 0\)
\(t \cdot e^{at}\) \(\frac{1}{(s - a)^2}\), \(s > a\)
\(t^n \cdot e^{at}\), \(n \in \mathbb{N}\) \(\frac{n!}{(s - a)^{n+1}}\), \(s > a\)
\(e^{-at} \cdot \sin(bt)\) \(\frac{b}{(s + a)^2 + b^2}\), \(s > -a\)
\(e^{-at} \cdot \cos(bt)\) \(\frac{s + a}{(s + a)^2 + b^2}\), \(s > -a\)
\(\sinh(at)\) \(\frac{a}{s^2 - a^2}\), \(s > |a|\)
\(\cosh(at)\) \(\frac{s}{s^2 - a^2}\), \(s > |a|\)
\(\delta(t - t_0)\) (Hàm Dirac) \(e^{-st_0}\)
Hàm bước \(u(t - a)\) \(\frac{e^{-as}}{s}\), \(s > 0\)
\(\frac{1}{\sqrt{t}}\) \(\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{s}}\), \(s > 0\)
\(\frac{t^{n-1}}{\Gamma(n)}\) \(\frac{1}{s^n}\), \(s > 0, n > 0\)
